WebGL + DOM Layer Structure for `/lusion`

Here is how the page layers stack and communicate locally and in Webflow.

Browser Viewport (Layer Stack)

1. DOM Layer (Visible Text & Layout)

Standard HTML tags for SEO and structured flow. Contains invisible anchor boxes (e.g. #home-reel-thumb) that specify coordinates for the WebGL graphics.

2. Fixed WebGL Canvas Layer (Three.js Render)

Covers 100% of the viewport behind or above the text. Renders the 3D drawing line spline and the morphing thumbnail video player.

3. Lenis Scroll Controller

Monitors scroll event details. Feeds scroll coordinates into the JS loops to sync the drawing progress of the line and the video morph ratio.
